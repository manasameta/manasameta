HOW TO BUY?

Create wallet

download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free Desktop users. download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Get some BNB

have BNB in your wallet to switch to $DollarEscape.if you don't have any BNB,you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.
